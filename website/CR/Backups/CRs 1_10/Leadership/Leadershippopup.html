Sonal is the driving force behind ADROSONIC’s global operations, customer success, and talent transformation. With a strategic vision rooted in care and empathy, she established the Customer Success Office – integrating client feedback and talent acquisition to elevate service quality and champion continuous innovation. Sonal’s disciplined execution shapes ADROSONIC’s delivery culture through standardised processes, cross-functional collaboration and sustainable growth. She ensures the CEO’s vision is realised by aligning people, processes and technology, while also overseeing key initiatives across risk, compliance, process maturity and quality governance. Deeply passionate about creating impact, she also leads ADROSONIC’s CSR efforts-spearheading initiatives to bring quality education to underprivileged children. Outside of work, she enjoys music and playing the keyboard. Sonal lives in Mumbai with her husband and two sons.
